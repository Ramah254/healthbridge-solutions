User Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data from HealthBridge Solutions at any time.

To request deletion, email admin@healthbridgesolutions.net from the same email address you used with our services, using the subject line “Data Deletion Request”.

In your message, include your full name and the phone number or email associated with your account.

We will verify your identity and confirm once your data has been deleted, except where we must retain certain information for legal, regulatory, or security reasons.
