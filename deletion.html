User Data Deletion

If you have an account with HealthBridge Solutions or have shared personal information with us, you can request that we delete your data at any time.

To request deletion, please send an email to admin@healthbridgesolutions.net from the email address you used with our services, with the subject line “Data Deletion Request”.

In your message, include your full name, the phone number or email you used with us, and a brief note that you want your data deleted.

We will verify your identity and respond to your request. In most cases, your data will be deleted, except where we are required to keep certain information for legal, regulatory, or security reasons.

If you have any questions before making a deletion request, you can also contact us at admin@healthbridgesolutions.net.
