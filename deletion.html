Data Deletion Request

How to request deletion of your personal data from HealthBridge Solutions.

You can request deletion of your personal data from HealthBridge Solutions at any time. We take these requests seriously and will process them promptly.

How to request deletion

Email us at admin@healthbridgesolutions.net from the same email address you used with our services, using the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

In your message, please include:

  • Your full name
  • The phone number or email associated with your account
  • The name of the facility (if you are a hospital staff member)

What happens next

We will verify your identity and confirm once your data has been deleted — usually within five business days. We may need to retain certain information for legal, regulatory, or security reasons, in which case we will explain what is being kept and why.

Prefer to talk to a human? Message us on WhatsApp at +254 795 822 310 or call us during business hours.
