MCH System

Every mother on schedule.
Every baby on time.

Maternal and child health lives or dies on continuity. The MCH System tracks every mother and child through their full care journey — and gently reaches out before a visit is missed, not after.

Book a 20-min Demo How it works
What it does

Three jobs — the ones that usually fall through the cracks.

Your clinical team focuses on care. We make sure no mother or baby falls off the schedule.

ANC visits completed

Every expectant mother is reminded ahead of each ANC visit, in the language she's comfortable with. Missed appointments are flagged early — before they turn into high-risk gaps.

Immunisations on track

From birth through year five, every scheduled dose is tracked. Mothers hear from you when each immunisation is due — so children don't miss the protection they need.

PNC follow-ups that actually happen

The post-natal weeks are when the highest-risk gaps open. We reach mothers at home with check-ins and reminders — turning a fragile moment into continuous care.

A typical journey

From the first ANC visit to the last immunisation.

Here's what the system does in the background for a single mother and child — over five years, without your team lifting a finger.

1

First ANC visit

Mother registers at your facility. The system captures her details and sets the calendar for every follow-up to come.

2

ANC reminders

Each scheduled visit triggers a friendly reminder ahead of time. Missed visits are flagged the same day, so you can act.

3

Delivery & PNC

After delivery, the system shifts into post-natal mode — reaching out at home through the critical first six weeks.

4

Immunisations

Every scheduled dose, from birth onward, is tracked and reminded. Mother knows when. Baby gets what they need.

5

Year five & beyond

By the time the child completes the schedule, the family is your patient base — loyal, retained, and quietly bringing others.

How it works

Three steps. No new burden on your nurses.

The follow-up work happens automatically in the background — your MCH team focuses on care.

1

We connect with your MCH unit

We sit with you and your nurses, map your ANC, PNC, and immunisation flows, and set up the system to fit how your team already works.

2

We reach mothers on the channels they use

Reminders, follow-ups, and home check-ins go out automatically through messaging mothers already check daily — no app to download, no friction.

3

You see results in your monthly report

Each month, an owner-friendly report: ANC completion, immunisation coverage, who's on track, who needs a nudge. Clear, actionable, no dashboards.

Owner questions

What hospital owners ask us first

Will our nurses have more work to do?

No — less, actually. The follow-up calls and reminders that nurses currently squeeze into spare moments are done automatically. Your team is freed up to focus on the clinical work.

What languages do you reach mothers in?

We can reach mothers in the languages your patient base actually uses — English, Swahili, and local languages where relevant. We'll discuss what fits your facility during the demo.

How do you handle sensitive patient data?

Mother and child data is stored securely, never shared, and remains the property of your facility. We treat it with the seriousness MCH care demands.

Can we run MCH and OPD together?

Yes — many facilities do. The two systems complement each other and give you full retention coverage across outpatient and maternal-child care.
