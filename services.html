Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare automation solutions designed to transform your medical facility's operations and patient experience.

AI-Powered OPD Automation

Transform your Outpatient Department with intelligent digital systems that streamline patient registration, queue management, and appointment scheduling. Our AI-powered OPD automation reduces wait times, eliminates manual errors, and creates a seamless experience for both patients and staff.

  • Digital patient registration & check-in
  • Intelligent queue management system
  • Automated appointment scheduling
  • Real-time department analytics
  • Patient flow optimization
  • Integration with existing HMS
  • Multi-language support
  • Mobile app for patients

WhatsApp Business Automation

Engage patients 24/7 with AI-powered WhatsApp chatbots that handle appointment booking, reminders, test results delivery, and instant support. With over 90% of Kenyans using WhatsApp, this is the most effective way to reach and serve your patients.

  • 24/7 automated patient support
  • Appointment booking & rescheduling
  • Automated appointment reminders
  • Test results delivery
  • Prescription refill requests
  • FAQ handling & health information
  • Two-way communication
  • Multilingual chatbot support

Voice Agent Systems

Deploy intelligent voice assistants that handle appointment bookings, prescription refills, and patient inquiries using natural language processing. Our voice agents understand Kenyan accents and can communicate in multiple local languages, making healthcare access easier for everyone.

  • Natural language processing
  • Appointment booking via phone
  • Prescription refill automation
  • 24/7 patient inquiry handling
  • Multi-language support (Swahili, English)
  • Call routing & escalation
  • Voice recognition technology
  • Call analytics & reporting

SMS Automation

Reach every patient with bulk and two-way SMS systems for appointment reminders, health alerts, medication adherence, and emergency notifications. SMS remains the most reliable communication channel in Kenya, ensuring your messages reach patients regardless of internet connectivity.

  • Bulk SMS campaigns
  • Appointment reminders
  • Medication adherence alerts
  • Health tips & education
  • Emergency notifications
  • Two-way SMS communication
  • Automated follow-ups
  • Delivery reports & analytics

Digital Workflow Optimization

Streamline clinic and hospital operations with automated workflows that reduce wait times, eliminate paperwork, and improve staff productivity. Our digital workflow solutions connect departments, automate routine tasks, and provide real-time visibility into your operations.

  • Paperless patient records
  • Department integration
  • Automated task assignment
  • Digital prescription management
  • Inventory & pharmacy integration
  • Billing & payment automation
  • Staff scheduling optimization
  • Real-time operational dashboards

Healthcare Data Management

Secure, compliant patient records and reporting systems with advanced analytics for better decision-making. Our healthcare data management solutions ensure your patient information is protected, accessible, and actionable while meeting all regulatory requirements.
