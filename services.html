Our Systems

Two systems built around a single outcome: patients who come back. Pick the one that matches the leak in your facility — or run both for full coverage.

For Outpatient Departments

OPD System

Turn one-time visits into long-term patients.

Keep your outpatients engaged after they leave — with timely follow-ups, review reminders, and a record of their care that's there the next time they walk in.

  • More returning patients through automated home follow-ups.
  • Fewer no-shows through reminders on the channels patients already use.
  • Continuity of care — returning patients are recognised instantly.
See the OPD System
For Maternal & Child Health

MCH System

Every mother on schedule. Every baby on time.

Track every mother and child through their full care journey — flagging missed ANC visits, keeping immunisations on schedule, and reaching mothers at home with PNC follow-ups.

  • ANC completion through reminders and home follow-ups.
  • Immunisation coverage tracked from birth through year five.
  • PNC follow-ups — the visits that usually fall through the cracks.
