OPD System

Turn one-time visits into long-term patients.

Most outpatient revenue isn't lost during the visit. It's lost in the silence after. Our OPD System keeps your patients engaged once they leave — so the next visit isn't a hope, it's a plan.

What it does

Three jobs — done automatically, in the background.

Your staff don't learn a new system. Your patients don't download an app. The work just gets done.

Brings patients back for reviews

Automated follow-ups reach patients at home and prompt them to return for their review — the visit that usually slips through the cracks. More returning patients without more nurse hours.

Cuts no-shows with timely reminders

Reminders go out before each scheduled appointment on the channels your patients actually check. Fewer empty consultation slots, fewer wasted clinical hours.

Recognises returning patients instantly

No re-registration friction. When a patient walks back in, their history is ready, their care continues smoothly, and your front desk doesn't grind to a halt.

What changes after install

Two views. Both improve.

The owner view

  • Higher visit-per-patient ratio across the year
  • Fewer empty consultation slots from no-shows
  • A clear monthly picture of who came back and who didn't
  • A defensible patient base — harder for competitors to poach

The patient view

  • Feels cared for after the visit, not just during it
  • Gets a friendly nudge before they forget the review
  • Walks back into a hospital that remembers them
  • Tells other patients — the cheapest marketing you'll ever do
How it works

Three steps. No IT department required.

Your team keeps running the hospital. We handle the follow-up work behind the scenes.

1

We connect with your facility

We meet with you and your clinical team, learn how your OPD flows, and set the system up to fit your workflow — not the other way around.

2

3

Owner questions

What hospital owners ask us first

Will my staff have to learn something new?

No. The OPD System works in the background. Your front desk and clinicians keep doing what they already do — the follow-up work happens without them touching anything new.

How long until we see results?

Setup typically takes a week. You'll see the first returning patients within the first month, and a clear retention pattern by month two or three.

What happens to our patient data?

Your data stays yours. It's stored securely, never shared, and you can export or delete it at any time. We treat patient information with the same seriousness your clinical team does.

Do you work with our existing systems?

Yes. We design the integration to fit how your facility already runs — not the reverse. We'll cover this in the demo, with your real workflow on the table.
