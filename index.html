For Outpatient Departments

OPD System

Turn one-time visits into long-term patients.

Most OPD revenue leaks between visits, not during them. Our OPD system keeps your patients engaged after they leave — with timely follow-ups, review reminders, and a record of their care that's there the next time they walk in.

  • More returning patients. Automated follow-ups on their health while they're at home.
  • Fewer no-shows. Appointment reminders that actually reach patients on the channels they use.
  • No re-registration friction. Returning patients are recognised instantly — their history is ready.
  • Monthly retention reports. The owner sees who came back, who didn't, and why.

For Maternal & Child Health

MCH System

Every mother on schedule. Every baby on time.

MCH care lives or dies on continuity. Our MCH system tracks every mother and child through their full care journey — flagging missed ANC visits, keeping immunisations on schedule, and following up at home where the highest-risk gaps usually open.

  • ANC visits completed. Mothers reminded and followed up at home, not just at the clinic.
  • Immunisations on track. From birth through year five, no child misses a scheduled dose.
  • PNC follow-ups that happen. The visits that usually fall through the cracks — covered.
  • A flagship MCH reputation. Mothers tell other mothers. Word-of-mouth becomes your best marketing.
