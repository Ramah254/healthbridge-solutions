Our Story

HealthBridge Solutions was founded in 2019 with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between healthcare and technology in Kenya. Our founder, Ramadhan Omar, recognized that while Kenya's healthcare sector was growing rapidly, many facilities were still struggling with outdated manual processes that hindered efficiency and patient care.

Starting from a small office in Athi River, Machakos, we began by developing custom software solutions for local clinics. Word spread quickly about the transformative impact of our automation systems, and soon we were partnering with hospitals and medical facilities across the country.

Today, HealthBridge Solutions has grown into one of Kenya's leading healthcare technology companies, serving over 50 healthcare partners and touching the lives of more than a million patients. But our mission remains the same: to empower healthcare providers with innovative technology that improves patient outcomes and operational efficiency.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower healthcare providers in Kenya and East Africa with innovative, affordable, and reliable technology solutions that enhance patient care, streamline operations, and improve health outcomes for all. We are committed to making advanced healthcare automation accessible to facilities of all sizes, from small clinics to large hospitals.

Our Vision

To be the leading healthcare technology partner in Africa, recognized for transforming how healthcare is delivered through cutting-edge automation, AI, and digital solutions. We envision a future where every healthcare facility in Kenya has access to world-class technology that enables them to provide exceptional patient care efficiently and affordably.
