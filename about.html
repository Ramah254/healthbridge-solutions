About HealthBridge

Built so Kenyan private hospitals stop losing patients.

HealthBridge Solutions is a Kenya-based company focused on one thing: helping private hospitals keep more of the patients they already attract. We do it through two systems — for MCH and OPD — that quietly handle the follow-up work your team doesn't have hours for.

From the founder

Why I started HealthBridge

I spent years watching something quietly painful in Kenyan private hospitals. Brilliant clinical teams, real care, real outcomes inside the consultation room — and then the patient walks out the door, and is never heard from again.

Not because the care was poor. Because no one had time to follow up. Nurses are already stretched. Front desks are already full. The review visit, the next ANC check, the immunisation due in two weeks — these don't get forgotten on purpose. They get forgotten because the work that would catch them isn't anyone's job.

HealthBridge exists to make that work happen automatically. Our MCH and OPD systems do the patient follow-up your team doesn't have hours for — quietly, in the background, in the language your patients speak. The result is simple: more patients come back.

Ramadhan Omar
Founder, HealthBridge Solutions
What we believe

Four ideas that shape everything we build.

We won't have wall-to-wall client logos for a while. What we do have is a clear point of view.

01

Retention beats acquisition

Private hospitals spend on attracting patients but lose them quietly between visits. Closing that gap is more profitable than buying more first visits.

02

Built here, for here

Kenyan private hospitals have specific workflows, languages, and patient habits. We design for those realities — not generic global templates.

03

Owners deserve plain numbers

No dashboards to learn. Each month you get a clear report: who came back, who didn't, what changed. The numbers an owner can actually act on.

04

The system should disappear

Good technology stays out of the way. Your staff shouldn't have to learn a new tool. Your patients shouldn't have to install an app. The work just gets done.
