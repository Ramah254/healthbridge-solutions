What's in your monthly report

The metrics that matter to an owner.

Every metric below is something you'll see in your inbox each month, in plain language — alongside a short note on what changed and where to focus next.

Returning patient rate

The percentage of first-time patients who came back for a second visit. The single most important number for OPD revenue.

No-show rate

Scheduled appointments that didn't happen. Tracked over time so you can see your reminder system working.

ANC completion rate

The share of expectant mothers who completed their full ANC schedule. A direct reflection of your MCH quality and continuity.

Immunisation coverage

Children in your patient base who are on schedule for their immunisations — and who needs a nudge this month.

PNC follow-up reach

The percentage of new mothers who received and engaged with post-natal check-ins through the critical first six weeks.

Patients slipping away

Patients who haven't returned in a defined window — flagged so you can decide whether to act before a competitor does.
