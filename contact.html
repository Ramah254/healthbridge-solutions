Common questions

What hospital owners ask us first

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on your facility size and whether you run MCH, OPD, or both. We'll quote you transparently during the demo — no hidden fees, no per-patient surprises.

How long until we're up and running?

Most facilities are live within one to two weeks. You'll start seeing patient follow-ups go out within the first month.

Will our staff have to learn complicated software?

No. The systems work in the background. Your team keeps using what they already use — we handle the follow-up work invisibly.

What happens to our patient data?

Your data is stored securely and remains your property. It is never sold, shared, or used for anything outside your facility. You can export or delete it at any time.

Is the training included?

Yes. Onsite training for your team is part of every engagement. Plus ongoing support over WhatsApp whenever you need us.

Do we have to commit long-term?

No long lock-ins. If the system isn't moving your retention numbers, you're free to walk away. We earn the relationship monthly.
