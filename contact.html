Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process.

How long does implementation take?

Implementation time varies based on the solution and facility size. Simple solutions like SMS automation can be deployed in 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive OPD automation typically takes 4-6 weeks including training.

Do you provide training for staff?

Yes, comprehensive training is included with all our solutions. We provide both on-site and remote training sessions to ensure your staff is comfortable using the new systems.

Is patient data secure?

Absolutely. We use end-to-end encryption, secure cloud infrastructure, and comply with international healthcare data standards. Your patient data is protected with the highest security measures.

Can you integrate with our existing systems?

Yes, our solutions are designed to integrate with most existing hospital management systems, laboratory information systems, and other healthcare software you may be using.

What support do you offer after implementation?

We provide 24/7 technical support, regular system updates, and ongoing optimization. Our Kenya-based support team is always available to assist you.

Do you work with small clinics?

Yes, we have solutions designed for facilities of all sizes, from individual practitioner clinics to large hospital networks. Our pricing is flexible to accommodate different budgets.

Prefer to Call?

Speak directly with our team. We're available Monday through Saturday to answer your questions and discuss your needs.
